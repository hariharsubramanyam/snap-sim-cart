SnapSim

An interactive simulation of the challenges a family on SNAP benefits may face.

Your name is Alexa Hansen.

You are a 32 year old widowed mother from Ridgeport, Mississippi.

After your husband died three months ago, you moved into a small 2-bedroom apartment with her two children, Paul and Jane.

You've recently got a job as a waitress at a local restaurant. It does not pay very well, but it is your primary source of income. You start work at 8AM and leave for home at 6PM.

Your children:

Paul Hansen

Paul is an energetic four-year-old who has just started preschool.

Things have been especially tough for him now because he lost his father and because you have to work so often.

Jane Hansen

Jane is a bright 14 year old who is a straight-A student and loves theater.

She had hoped to join the school's theater club, but could not because she babysits her younger brother after school each day.

The Problem:

Today, you've has gone to Greenshop, the local supermarket, after spending the day working. You have picked up a few groceries and hope to return home to spend a little time with her kids before they go to sleep.

However, the cost of the groceries you've selected exceed the amount of money in your budget, which is provided by SNAP.

You must remove some items from your cart until you are able to pay for what is left.
