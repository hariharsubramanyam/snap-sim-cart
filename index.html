See some of the sacrifices that a family of three on SNAP may have to make.

Meet Alexa

Alexa Hansen

Alexa Hansen is a 32 year old widowed mother from Ridgeport, Mississippi.

After her husband died three months ago, she moved into a small 2-bedroom apartment with her two children, Paul and Jane.

She recently got a job as a waitress at a local restaurant. It does not pay very well, but it is her primary source of income. She starts work at 8AM to and leaves at 6PM.

And her kids

Paul Hansen

Paul is an energetic four-year-old who has just started preschool.

Things have been especially tough for him now because he lost his father and because his mother has to work so often.

Jane Hansen

Jane is a bright 14 year old who is a straight-A student and loves theater.

She had hoped to join the school's theater club, but could not because she babysits her younger brother after school each day.

Your job

Today, Alexa has gone to Greenshop, the local supermarket, after spending the day working. She will buy groceries and hopes to return home to spend a little time with her kids before they go to sleep.

Your job is to help her select which items to keep in her cart so that she stays within her SNAP budget.

As you clear the cart, look at why Alexa has chosen each item, and pay attention to the sacrifices being made.
